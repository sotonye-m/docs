---
title: "Submit Business KYB"
description: "Submit identity and compliance verification data for a business customer"
---

Submit KYB (Know Your Business) data for a business customer. The customer must already exist with `customer_type: "business"`.

```
POST https://kyc.yativo.com/api/business-kyc/submit
```

<Note>
  This endpoint is on the KYC platform (`https://kyc.yativo.com`). Requires `Idempotency-Key` header.
</Note>

## Business identity fields

<ParamField body="customer_id" type="string" required>
  The customer UUID from `POST /customer`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="type" type="string" required>
  Must be `"business"`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="business_legal_name" type="string" required>
  The official registered legal name of the business.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="business_description" type="string" required>
  Description of what the business does.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="email" type="string" required>
  Business contact email address.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="business_type" type="string" required>
  One of: `"cooperative"`, `"corporation"`, `"llc"`, `"partnership"`, `"sole_prop"`, `"trust"`, `"other"`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="registration_number" type="string" required>
  Official government registration or incorporation number.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="incorporation_date" type="string" required>
  Date of incorporation in ISO 8601 format (e.g. `"2015-06-15"`).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="business_trade_name" type="string">
  Trade name or DBA (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="tax_id" type="string">
  Business tax identification number (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="statement_descriptor" type="string">
  How the business name appears on customer statements (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="phone_calling_code" type="string">
  Country calling code for business phone (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="phone_number" type="string">
  Business phone number (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="business_industry" type="string">
  Industry category (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="primary_website" type="string">
  Business website URL (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="is_dao" type="boolean">
  Whether the business is a DAO (optional).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="has_material_intermediary_ownership" type="boolean">
  Whether there is material intermediary ownership (optional).
</ParamField>
